Our workflow

Every process is tailor made

to best suit your needs.

We help you digitize your MCA processes and automate the manual steps from intake steps, auto-decisioning with artificial intelligence, functional due-diligence, integrated e-sign documentation, automated scheduled transactions, and intuitive payment tracking.

Add Deal into CRM

Our internal computer vision machine learning algorithm analyzes your ISO documents and automatically organizes it into our system.

Pre-Fund

A preliminary leverage score is calculated for each merchant profile based on the initial bank statements provided by the ISO.

In this pre-funding stage each merchant profile is on stand-by for further review.

Set Terms and Begin Underwriting

Once you decide to fund a merchant, you can easily set the terms and automatically propagate and send an e-signature document with the desired terms.

Once a document is sent, our system will auto begin the due-diligence process by acquiring and synthesizing all the necessary underwriting information.

Full Underwriting

Our system elegantly lays out all the essential underwriting information. This includes 3 years of bank statements, a full and up to date credit report, and cohesive business background check.

We lay out all the critical data that you need to make an informative funding decision.

Set Syndicators and Fund

Once you decide to fund a merchant then you simply edit your initial terms (if necessary), set your desired syndicators, and click fund.

The initial fund amount will be automatically sent to the merchants desired bank account and tracking will commence.

Recieve Payment and Track Merchant

Based on your selected repayment schedule, our system will automatically withdraw a set amount from each merchant's bank account.

The data associated with each merchant's funding is simply and efficiently displayed for you to easily capture the repayment's progress.
